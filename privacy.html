UpliftMe Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2025

App: UpliftMe

Developer / Publisher: Noble Edge Digital

1. Introduction

UpliftMe is a wellness and journaling app developed by Noble Edge Digital. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

2. Information We Collect

a. Information You Provide

  • Journal entries, notes, reflections, and other content you create in the app
  • Optional feedback you send to us by email or through support communications

b. Automatically Collected Information

  • Basic device information (such as device model and operating system version)
  • App usage and performance information
  • Crash logs used to diagnose technical issues

We do not intentionally collect precise location information, financial information, or contact lists from your device.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the UpliftMe app
  • Save and display your journal entries and other in-app content
  • Understand app performance and fix bugs or crashes
  • Respond to your support requests and feedback

Your journal content is used only to provide the app’s core functionality and is not sold or used for third-party advertising.

4. Legal Basis (Where Applicable)

Where required by applicable law, we process your information based on one or more of the following legal bases:

  • Your consent, where you choose to provide information or use optional features
  • Our legitimate interest in providing and improving the app
  • Compliance with legal obligations

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information.

We may share limited information in the following situations:

  • With service providers that help us operate the app (for example, crash reporting or analytics tools), who are required to protect your data and use it only for the services they provide to us
  • To comply with applicable laws, regulations, legal processes, or enforceable governmental requests
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Noble Edge Digital, our users, or others

6. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to help protect your information, including secure storage and limited access controls. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your information for as long as needed to provide the UpliftMe service and for legitimate business or legal purposes.

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at upliftmeapp.support@gmail.com with the subject line “Delete My Data.” We will delete your personal data and journal content within a reasonable time, unless we are required to retain it longer for legal or operational reasons.

8. Children’s Privacy

UpliftMe is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it.

9. International Users

Your information may be processed and stored in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate. Data protection laws in these locations may be different from those in your country of residence.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the “Effective Date” at the top of this page. Your continued use of UpliftMe after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.
