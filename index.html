Frequently Asked Questions

I can’t save or see my journal entries.

Make sure you are using the latest version of UpliftMe from the App Store. If the issue continues, please email support with a description of what happens and, if possible, a screenshot.

How can I request deletion of my data?

You can request deletion of your data at any time by emailing upliftmeapp.support@gmail.com with the subject line “Delete My Data.” We will process your request within a reasonable time.

Is my information private?

Your journal entries and reflections are private and are used only to provide the core functionality of the app. For more details, please review our Privacy Policy.

About UpliftMe

UpliftMe is a wellness and journaling app focused on reflection, encouragement, and mental well-being. The app is developed and published by Noble Edge Digital, a digital studio creating simple, supportive tools for everyday life.

UpliftMe is not a medical or mental health service and does not provide professional advice. If you are experiencing a crisis or mental health emergency, please contact your local emergency number or a qualified professional.
